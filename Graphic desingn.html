Graphic Desingn

Discover your creative potential with a Graphic Desingn today.

Degree-level UK graphic design learning with Educare College

The combination of typography, image, and layout are the basic visual building blocks of graphic design.

Study of this design discipline focuses on exploring visual language and applying creative problem solving to deliver visual solutions that communicate effectively. Through defining your own visual language you’ll be able to lead self-directed projects, work with clients, and produce creative design solutions to complex problems and briefs.

Specialise by studying our BA (Hons) Graphic Design degree programme or combine your study in graphic design with illustration and other design disciplines in the BA (Hons) Visual Communications degree programme.



What are graphic design courses?

Our courses for graphic design are totally flexible, meaning that you can study at a pace that suits you. So, if you want a programme that fits around your life, one of our UK graphic design courses could be for you!

Many people choose online learning graphic design courses so that they can continue to manage their personal or professional commitments alongside their studies. This means that our students can continue working, parenting or caring while they develop their skills through our graphic design courses.

In Myanmar, we are facing a crisis situation and we had a group of motivated individuals who recognized the potential of the young generation in the creative field. They see the need for a platform that fosters a positive and supportive learning environment and empowers young people with the skills and tools they need. Thus, the idea of Myanmar's first art department was born.

Educare College is a place where young people can grow and thrive with love and care. The founders believe that by inculcating values such as empathy and compassion, young people will not only succeed in their professional lives but also make a positive impact on society.

The brand story of Educare College is hope, One of inspiration and transformation. We want to create a space where youth can unleash their creativity and explore their potential. We believe that each person has a unique voice and our goal is to provide a platform where that voice can be heard.

If you’re looking for our courses for beginners – our degrees are open access, this means you won’t need any formal qualifications to apply.
