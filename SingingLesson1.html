Singing Lesson1

What you'll learn

Learn how to sing with our music & singing courses in Educare College!

In our singing lessons for beginners, basic techniques of voice production are explored in a group setting. The aim is to help students develop vocal freedom and increase their confidence in their voice and how to use it. Singers will learn how to utilise the whole body efficiently to make singing easier and more enjoyable!

This fun eight-week course combines the practical with the technical - all designed to help you make the most of your voice. The first four weeks will be spent learning basic techniques, while the second four weeks will be focused on performance skills with an aim for each student to perform an individual piece of their choosing in the final week. The classes are more than a singalong group - students will learn solid vocal concepts covered in private singing lessons.

Each class has:

  • Technical/musical ideas to explore and learn
  • A chance to ask questions about singing
  • Practical application of class ideas
  • Singing of songs in a group setting

    • Learning outcomes

    By the end of this singing course, you should be able to:

  • Demonstrate a balanced and efficient posture for singing
  • Understand the basic principles of vocal production that give vocal freedom
  • Connect the idea of good breathing to posture
  • Understand and demonstrate the emotional interpretation and musicality within songs
  • Display greater confidence in your voice and using it to perform
  • Understand the basic principles of scales and harmony

    • Course content

    What will be covered in this singing course?

  • Sing assigned vocal exercises; learn about basic voice function
  • Prepare assigned repertoire for class, solo and group performance opportunities
  • Learn repertoire demonstrating various styles
  • Practicing consistently to develop good vocal habits and increase confidence
